This is not just a game design class. We move from understanding AI-driven risks to evaluating existing games, and finally building evidence-based solutions[cite: 6, 11]. You will leave as an intervention-minded professional[cite: 9].

  • Analyze: Deepfakes, LLM hallucination, and synthetic media[cite: 22].
  • Play: Critically evaluate "Bad News," "Cranky Uncle," and more[cite: 26].
  • Create: Prototype a high-fidelity game intervention[cite: 33].
